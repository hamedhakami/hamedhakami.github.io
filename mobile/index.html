Key Visual for IranCard Yalda Campaign:
“The Trace of Brightness”

COMPANY

ERTEBAT FARDA (IRANCARD)

YEAR 2022

The main idea of this campaign was IranCard's financial assistance to poor people on the longest night of the year. Seeing the constellation of pomegranate (a symbolic fruit for Yalda night in the Persian culture) in the pitch darkness of the night is an indication of hope for the poor.
Pomegranate's gradual appearance among the night stars can be seen step by step when opening the package, with the final step being the revelation of the pomegranate's crimson glow in the sky.
